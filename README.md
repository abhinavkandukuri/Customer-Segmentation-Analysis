# Customer Segmentation Analysis using Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on customer segmentation analysis using the Mall Customers dataset. The objective is to identify distinct customer groups based on demographic and spending behavior characteristics, enabling businesses to develop targeted marketing strategies and improve customer engagement.

The dashboard was developed in Power BI and provides interactive visualizations to analyze customer demographics, income levels, spending patterns, and customer segments.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Analyze customer demographics and purchasing behavior.
* Segment customers based on income and spending patterns.
* Identify high-value and potential customer groups.
* Create an interactive dashboard for customer analytics.
* Generate business insights for targeted marketing strategies.

---

## 📊 Dataset Information

The dataset contains customer information collected from a shopping mall, including:

* Customer ID
* Gender
* Age
* Annual Income (k$)
* Spending Score (1–100)

## 👥 Customer Segments

Customers were categorized into four segments:

### High Value Customers

* High Income
* High Spending Score
* Ideal targets for premium products and loyalty programs

### Potential Customers

* High Income
* Moderate Spending Score
* Opportunity for personalized marketing campaigns

### Frequent Spenders

* Moderate Income
* High Spending Score
* Strong engagement and repeat purchasing behavior

### Budget Customers

* Lower Income
* Lower Spending Score
* Suitable for discount-based promotions

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* High Value Customers contribute significantly to overall spending.
* Potential Customers possess strong purchasing power and can be converted into premium customers.
* Frequent Spenders show high engagement despite moderate income levels.
* Spending behavior varies across different age groups.
* Customer segmentation enables more effective marketing and customer retention strategies.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendations

* Develop loyalty programs for High Value Customers.
* Launch targeted campaigns for Potential Customers.
* Offer personalized recommendations to Frequent Spenders.
* Implement cost-effective promotions for Budget Customers.
* Use customer segmentation to improve marketing ROI and customer satisfaction.
